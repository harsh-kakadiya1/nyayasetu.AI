Ask Questions About This Document

What notice period is required for termination?

According to Section 8.1, either party must provide 30 days written notice for termination. However, Section 8.2 allows the company to terminate immediately "for cause" or with 2 weeks notice for other reasons.

Recommended Actions

1

Review Termination Terms

Consider negotiating for a longer notice period or severance package to protect against sudden termination.

2

Clarify IP Ownership

Ensure your personal projects created before employment are explicitly excluded from company IP claims.

3

Legal Review Recommended

Given the broad termination clause and extended non-compete, consider having an employment attorney review this contract.
